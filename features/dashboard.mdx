The Dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of your C.A.R. 911 system with real-time statistics, equipment status tracking, and fleet distribution analytics. You can monitor equipment across all departments, divisions, and dependencies at a glance.

## Overview

The Dashboard serves as your command center, displaying:

- Real-time equipment counts by status (functional, non-functional, under review)
- Fleet distribution across departments and divisions
- Equipment by provider (Patagonia Green, Telecom, Policía de Entre Ríos)
- Stock levels and available equipment
- Partial uninstallations and equipment in transit

## Equipment Status Categories

The Dashboard tracks equipment in the following states:

<Accordion title="Functional Equipment">
  Equipment in operational condition:
  - **New**: Brand new equipment ready for deployment
  - **Used**: Equipment currently in active use
  - **Repaired**: Equipment that has been serviced and returned to operational status
  
  View counts by brand, model, category, and provider. See how many units are in stock vs. in active use.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Non-Functional Equipment">
  Equipment that is not operational:
  - Grouped by brand, model, and provider
  - Includes equipment awaiting repair
  - Shows total count of units requiring attention
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Decommissioned Equipment">
  Equipment removed from service:
  - **Decommissioned**: Permanently removed from inventory
  - **Lost**: Equipment reported as missing
  - **Replacement**: Units designated as spare parts
  
  Tracked separately to maintain accurate inventory records.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Equipment Under Review">
  Equipment undergoing evaluation or maintenance:
  - Shows units temporarily removed from active service
  - Groups by brand and model
  - Tracks count and provider information
</Accordion>

## Location-Based Views

### Stock 911
View all equipment currently in central stock ("Stock 911"):
- Equipment details (TEI, ISSI, brand, model)
- Assignment history
- Previous resource allocation
- Assignment dates and timestamps

### Departmental Distribution
Track equipment assigned to "Departamental Paraná (JDP)" and all subordinate units:
- Real-time equipment count
- Resource assignments
- Vehicle associations
- Equipment status filtering (excludes non-functional units)

### Division Views

<Steps>
  <Step title="División 911 y Videovigilancia">
    Monitor equipment assigned to the 911 and Video Surveillance Division, including all subordinate units and resources.
  </Step>
  <Step title="División Seguridad Urbana y Bancaria">
    Track equipment deployed to the Urban and Banking Security Division across all associated departments.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Patagonia Green Location
View equipment at the Patagonia Green facility:
- Equipment count and details
- Assignment records
- Resource allocations
- Historical tracking

## Provider Analytics

Track equipment by provider with detailed status breakdowns:

**Patagonia Green**
- Equipment grouped by status and model
- Total counts across all statuses
- Brand and model distribution

**Telecom**
- Provider-specific equipment tracking
- Status-based grouping
- Model and brand analytics

**Policía de Entre Ríos**
- Internal fleet tracking
- Complete status breakdown
- Equipment lifecycle monitoring

<Info>
  All provider views include both status-specific counts and total equipment counts for comprehensive tracking.
</Info>

## Vehicle and Resource Tracking

### Mobile Units
Track vehicles (cars and trucks) in the fleet:
- Vehicle type and details
- Resource name
- Assigned department
- Current location

### Motorcycles
Monitor motorcycle units separately:
- Complete motorcycle inventory
- Department assignments
- Resource tracking

## Partial Uninstallations

View equipment with partial uninstallation status:
- Most recent movement for each unit
- TEI and ISSI identification
- Assignment dates
- Movement type tracking

<Note>
  The Dashboard automatically refreshes data and shows only the latest status for each equipment unit. Historical data is accessible through the fleet management section.
</Note>

## Data Accuracy

The Dashboard uses caching mechanisms for optimal performance:
- Equipment status IDs are cached to reduce database queries
- Real-time calculations for equipment distribution
- Automatic exclusion of non-functional equipment from active counts
- Join queries across multiple tables for comprehensive views

## Quick Actions

From the Dashboard, you can quickly:
- Filter equipment by status, location, or provider
- View detailed equipment information
- Navigate to specific departments or divisions
- Export data for reporting
- Access equipment history and movement records
